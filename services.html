About Our Therapies

Abhyangam (Massage)

Abhyanga is a holistic treatment that nourishes your body with healing touch. It improves blood circulation, relieves pain, tension, and travel fatigue.

Shirodhara

An ancient therapy where warm medicated oil is poured in a continuous stream over the forehead ('third eye'). Excellent for stress, anxiety, depression, insomnia, and mental fatigue.

Potli Massage (Kizhi)

Uses small herbal pouches made of various herbs warmed in herbal oils. Great for osteoarthritis, arthritis with swelling, spondylosis, and sports injuries.

Pizhichil

Lukewarm medicated oil is applied to the body while lying on the pathi. The oil is squeezed by hand over the body at a defined height in a rhythmic style. A classic rejuvenation and anti-aging therapy.
